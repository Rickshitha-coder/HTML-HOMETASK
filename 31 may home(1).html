About Meesho

Meesho is India's largest and fastest-growing marketplace for entrepreneurs and small businesses. We empower individuals to launch their online stores with zero investment, giving them access to millions of customers across the country. Whether it’s fashion, electronics, home decor, or beauty products—Meesho makes it easy to start selling online with confidence.

Frequently Asked Question

FAQs

What is Meesho?

Meesho is a platform that enables small businesses and individuals to start their own online stores with zero investment.

How can I join?

You can join Meesho by downloading the app or visiting our website and signing up using your mobile number or email.

What products can I sell?

You can sell fashion, electronics, home decor, beauty products, and much more through Meesho’s marketplace.
