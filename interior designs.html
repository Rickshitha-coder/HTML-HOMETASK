Types of Interior Designs

1. Modern Interior Design
Characterized by clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and an emphasis on function over form. Modern interior design often features:
- Neutral color palette
- Industrial materials like steel and concrete
- Geometric shapes and abstract patterns

2. Luxury Interior Design
Designed to evoke opulence and sophistication, luxury interior design often features:
- Rich, bold colors and patterns
- High-end materials like marble, velvet, and crystal
- Ornate details and statement pieces

3.Minimalist Interior Design
Focused on simplicity and functionality, minimalist interior design often features:
- Monochromatic color scheme
- Limited ornamentation and decorative elements
- Clean lines and geometric shapes

4. Industrial Interior Design
Inspired by old factories and warehouses, industrial interior design often features:
- Exposed brick, concrete, and metal beams
- Industrial materials like metal and reclaimed wood
- Edison bulbs and other vintage lighting fixtures

5. Mid-Century Modern Interior Design
Characterized by retro flair and organic shapes, mid-century modern interior design often features:
- Warm, earthy color palette
- Iconic furniture pieces like Eames chairs and Noguchi coffee tables
- Graphic patterns and abstract artwork
